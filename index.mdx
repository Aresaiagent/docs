Ares is an AI operator for home-service businesses. It plugs into your CRM and your Meta ad account, then does the work a marketing hire would do: follows up with every lead, nurtures them toward a booked estimate, launches and monitors ad campaigns, and reports back to you in plain English.

You talk to Ares the way you would talk to an employee. It handles the busywork and asks for your approval before anything customer-visible or money-related goes out.

## How it fits together

1. You connect your CRM and your ad account once, during onboarding.
2. Ares monitors leads, conversations, and campaigns around the clock.
3. Routine work happens automatically. Sensitive work waits for your approval.
4. You check the dashboard or ask Ares directly whenever you want the current picture.
