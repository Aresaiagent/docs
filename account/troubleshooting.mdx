## A connection shows as disconnected

Authorizations expire or get revoked upstream from time to time.

1. Open the connections panel on your dashboard.
2. Choose the affected connection and reconnect with the same account you used originally.
3. Ares resumes automatically once the connection is healthy. Nothing is lost while disconnected; work queues up and catches up.

## Leads are not showing up

- Check that your CRM connection is healthy on the dashboard.
- If the missing leads come from a website form, submit a test lead yourself and ask Ares in chat whether it arrived.
- If the missing leads come from ads, ask Ares to check the campaign's lead form.

Ares can usually tell you exactly where a specific lead stalled: ask it by name.

## Messages are not sending

- Confirm the lead has not opted out; opt-outs are honored permanently.
- New texting numbers go through carrier registration, which can take a few business days after setup. Ares will tell you if your number is still pending.

## A campaign looks wrong

Ask Ares in chat first: "why is cost per lead up this week?" It will pull the numbers and explain. If something needs changing, it will propose the change through your approval queue.

## Sign-in problems

Use the email address from your original checkout. If you signed up with Google, keep using Google sign-in. Still stuck? Book a call at [calendly.com/contact-aresdeploy/ares-demo](https://calendly.com/contact-aresdeploy/ares-demo) and we will fix it live.
