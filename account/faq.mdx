---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Common questions about Ares."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need my own HighLevel or Meta account?">
    You need a Meta ad account to run ads. For the CRM, connect your existing HighLevel account during onboarding; if you do not have one, book a setup call and we will get you sorted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will leads know they are talking to an AI?">
    Ares communicates as your team's assistant, in your business's voice. Messages are short, human, and focused on getting the estimate booked. You can review exactly what goes out through your approval settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Ares spend my money without asking?">
    No. Every campaign launch and every budget change is previewed and requires your explicit confirmation. See [Approvals](/using/approvals).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Everything lives in your CRM and your ad account, which you own. Cancel and it all stays where it is. Ares never deletes contacts under any circumstances.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What trades does Ares work for?">
    Ares is built for home-service businesses: concrete coatings, hardscaping, electrical, roofing, and similar trades where the sale happens at an estimate. If you book estimates, Ares fits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?">
    An agency runs your ads and hands you leads. Ares runs your ads, answers the leads, follows up for weeks, books the estimate, and keeps your CRM clean, all day, every day, for a flat monthly price.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I still use my CRM myself?">
    Yes. Ares works inside your CRM alongside you and your team. Anything you do manually, Ares sees and works around.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
