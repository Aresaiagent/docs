## Your subscription

Ares is a monthly subscription purchased at [aresdeploy.com](https://aresdeploy.com). Your plan covers your AI operator, the dashboard, and the CRM and ads connections.

## Managing your plan

Manage your subscription, payment method, and invoices from the billing section of your dashboard at [app.aresdeploy.com](https://app.aresdeploy.com).

## Ad spend is separate

Your subscription pays for Ares. Ad spend goes directly from your card on file with Meta to Meta, at whatever budget you approve. Ares never holds or marks up your ad budget, and it never increases spend without your approval.

## Canceling

You can cancel any time from billing. Your operator keeps working until the end of the paid period, your data stays in your CRM, and nothing gets deleted.
