## 1. Create your account

Purchase a plan at [aresdeploy.com](https://aresdeploy.com). When checkout completes you are signed in to your dashboard at [app.aresdeploy.com](https://app.aresdeploy.com) automatically. You can also sign in later with the email you used at checkout.

## 2. Run the onboarding wizard

The first time you open the dashboard, Ares walks you through setup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Link your HighLevel account so Ares can see contacts, conversations, pipelines, and calendars. See [CRM connection](/connections/crm) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your ad account">
    Link your Meta ad account so Ares can launch and monitor campaigns. See [Ads connection](/connections/ads).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meet your operator">
    Open the chat and tell Ares about your business: your trade, your service area, and what a good lead looks like for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can skip a connection and come back to it later from the dashboard.

## 3. Let it run

Once connected, Ares starts monitoring. New leads get followed up, conversations get tracked, and anything that needs your judgment lands in the [approval queue](/using/approvals).
