title: "CRM connection"

---

Ares uses your CRM as its home base for lead handling. Connecting it is the single most important setup step.

## What Ares can do once connected

- Read and update contacts, tags, and custom fields
- Read conversations across SMS and email
- Send follow-up messages to leads, subject to your [approval settings](/using/approvals)
- Move opportunities through your pipeline as leads progress
- Check calendars and work toward booked appointments

## What Ares will not do

- It never deletes contacts. Ever.
- It never messages outside the guardrails you set.
- It never touches billing or account settings inside your CRM.

## Connecting

1. Open [app.aresdeploy.com](https://app.aresdeploy.com) and go to the connections step of onboarding, or the connections panel on the dashboard.
2. Choose your CRM and authorize access to the account you use for your business.
3. Ares confirms the connection and reads in your pipelines and calendars.

<Note>
Keep your pipeline simple. Ares works best with a handful of clear stages that start at the booking, not a dozen micro-stages. Fewer stages means cleaner reporting and fewer misfiled leads.
</Note>

## Disconnecting

You can revoke access at any time from the connections panel. Ares stops all CRM activity immediately and your data stays in your CRM, untouched.
