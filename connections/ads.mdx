---
title: "Ads connection"
description: "Connect your Meta ad account so Ares can launch and monitor lead campaigns."
---

With your ad account connected, Ares becomes your media buyer: it launches lead-generation campaigns, keeps an eye on spend and results, and tells you when something needs a decision.

## What Ares can do once connected

- Launch lead-generation campaigns with instant forms on Facebook and Instagram
- Target the cities and service areas you specify
- Pull in new leads from your ads and start follow-up right away
- Report spend, cost per lead, and lead volume on your dashboard
- Pause campaigns that are wasting budget

## Spend safety

Ad spend is treated as a sensitive action:

- Ares never launches a campaign without showing you a preview and getting your explicit confirmation.
- Ad copy is either written by you or drafted by Ares and confirmed by you before it runs. Nothing goes live unseen.
- Budgets are set by you. Ares works within them and flags anything unusual.

## Connecting

1. Open the connections step in onboarding, or the connections panel on the dashboard.
2. Choose the ads connection and sign in with the Meta account that owns your ad account.
3. Pick the ad account and page Ares should use.

## Disconnecting

Revoke access from the connections panel at any time. Running campaigns stay under your control in Meta Ads Manager; Ares simply stops managing them.
