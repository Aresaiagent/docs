title: "Chat with Ares"
description: "Talk to your operator in plain English. Ask questions, give instructions, change direction."
---

Chat is how you manage Ares. There are no forms to fill out and no settings maze: you tell it what you want and it does the work or tells you what it needs.

## Things owners ask every day

**Status and reporting**

- "How many leads came in this week?"
- "What did we spend on ads yesterday and what did it get us?"
- "Show me everyone who has not been followed up with."

**Instructions**

- "Follow up with everyone who filled out the form but never booked."
- "Pause the campaign in Fresno."
- "Draft a new ad for our spring promotion and show me before anything runs."

**Business context**

- "We are booked out three weeks. Slow the ads down."
- "We just added patio covers as a service. Update how you talk to leads."

## How Ares responds

Ares answers with specifics: real counts, real names, real numbers. When you ask for something that requires a sensitive action, like sending messages or changing ad spend, it prepares the work and routes it through [approvals](/using/approvals) instead of firing immediately.

<Tip>
Give Ares context the way you would brief a new hire. The more it knows about your trade, your area, and what a good customer looks like, the sharper its judgment gets.
</Tip>
