Speed to lead wins jobs. Ares exists to make sure no lead sits, no follow-up gets forgotten, and every conversation moves toward a booked estimate.

## The lifecycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Capture">
    A lead comes in from your ads, your website forms, or directly into your CRM. Ares sees it immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="First touch">
    The lead gets a fast, personal-sounding response while your competitors are still checking voicemail.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Nurture">
    No reply? Ares follows up on a schedule that persists for weeks without being annoying. Replies get handled in context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book">
    The goal of every conversation is a booked estimate on your calendar. Ares drives toward that and updates your pipeline when it lands.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ground rules baked into Ares

- **The objective is the booking.** Ares does not quote prices over text. Pricing questions get steered to the estimate, where you close on your terms.
- **Every message is deduplicated.** No lead ever gets the same message twice or gets double-texted by overlapping sequences.
- **Opt-outs are honored instantly** and logged in your CRM.
- **Leads are never deleted.** Cold leads get tagged and parked, not thrown away.

## What you see

The dashboard shows lead volume, response status, and who is moving through the pipeline. Ask Ares in chat for anything deeper: "who went quiet this week?" gets you names, not a chart.
