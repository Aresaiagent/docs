The dashboard at [app.aresdeploy.com](https://app.aresdeploy.com) is where you check on your business in under a minute. It is built to answer three questions: what came in, what is Ares doing about it, and what needs me.

## What you will see

- **Leads**: new lead volume over time, including which leads came from your ads.
- **Campaign performance**: spend, cost per lead, and the areas your ads are targeting.
- **Attention items**: anything Ares could not resolve on its own, surfaced with context so you can decide fast.
- **Approvals**: pending actions waiting on your sign-off. See [Approvals](/using/approvals).
- **Chat**: the conversation with your operator, always one click away.

## The daily rhythm

Most owners use Ares like this:

1. Open the dashboard with your morning coffee.
2. Clear the approval queue: a few taps.
3. Skim the lead and campaign numbers.
4. Ask Ares anything that looks off: "why did leads dip yesterday?"

That is the whole routine. Ares handles the rest of the day on its own.
